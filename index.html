Frequently Asked

I've never done Pilates before — is this for me?

Absolutely. All Auric Movement events are open to every level. The instructor guides every movement with clear cues, modifications, and zero judgment. Come curious.

Is the instructor certified?

Yes. Auric Movement's lead instructor holds a Comprehensive Pilates Instructor certification — 500+ hours of in-depth study in anatomy, the full Pilates repertoire (mat, reformer, cadillac, chair, barrels), and teaching methodology. Comprehensive certification is widely considered the gold standard of Pilates training.

What kind of Pilates can I expect?

Auric Movement classes are rooted in contemporary Pilates with deep respect for the classical method. Expect the foundational Pilates repertoire — mat and reformer work, breath-led movement, controlled flow — refined with modern principles of biomechanics so the practice meets your body where it is. Classes are intentional, mindful, and build strength, mobility, and presence over time.

What should I wear and bring?

It is advised to wear comfortable workout attire. If you need to bring a mat and/or grip socks for a certain location, it will be disclosed in your confirmation email.

Can the class be modified for an injury, pregnancy, or postpartum?

Yes — the health disclosure on the booking form lets you tell me what's going on so I can prepare modifications in advance. If you're navigating something specific (recent surgery, late-term pregnancy, etc.), please email me beforehand at auricmovement@outlook.com so we can plan together.

Where are the events held?

Auric Movement events are hosted at carefully sourced venues across New York City — the location varies by event. Each space is chosen for its atmosphere, light, and intentionality.

The specific address, arrival instructions, and any access codes are included in your registration confirmation and reminder emails. If you have a venue access question before booking, email auricmovement@outlook.com.

What if I arrive late?

There's a 5-minute grace period only. Out of respect for the group and the practice, late arrivals can't be admitted and refunds aren't issued. Please plan to arrive at your scheduled arrival time.

What's the cancellation and transfer policy?

Pilates x Niara (June 19, 2026): Refunds are available within 24 hours of purchase only. Spot transfers to a friend can be arranged up to 4 hours before your session.

Mat & Chat and all future events: All sales are final. Cancel at least 48 hours before the event and we’ll issue a credit for the full amount you paid, valid toward any future Auric Movement event hosted by Niara (collaborations and partner events excluded) for 6 months. Cancellations within 48 hours or no-shows are not eligible for a credit. Spot transfers to a friend are welcome up to 24 hours before the event — email auricmovement@outlook.com with both names.

To cancel, email auricmovement@outlook.com with your full name and session details.

How do I know my spot is confirmed?

You reserve your spot and pay right on the booking page. Once your payment is confirmed, you'll receive a confirmation email with your unique confirmation code — that's how you know your spot is officially secured. A reminder email then goes out the day before your session with all the details you need.

Don't see it? Check your spam or promotions folder — sometimes our emails land there. If it's still missing, email auricmovement@outlook.com and we'll resend.

Is there a waitlist if an event is full?

Yes. If a session is sold out, you can join the waitlist on the booking page. If a spot opens, you'll get an email with a window to claim it before it rolls to the next person.

Do I need to sign a waiver?

Yes — you'll sign the Auric Movement waiver as part of the booking flow.

Are the venues accessible?

If you have specific accessibility needs (mobility, hearing, vision, sensory), please email auricmovement@outlook.com before booking and we'll confirm what each venue can support and how we can make the experience work for you.

How can I host a corporate or private event?

Auric Movement hosts private and corporate Pilates and wellness sessions for groups. Head to the Private, Group & Corporate Events tab to send details about your group and what you're imagining.

Still have a question? Email auricmovement@outlook.com and we'll get back to you.

Your information is used solely to: (a) confirm your registration; (b) send event communications; (c) notify waitlisted participants when a spot opens; (d) maintain a record of signed liability waivers; (e) process cancellations; and (f) allow your instructor to safely modify activity based on health disclosures.

Registration data is stored via Supabase (a secure third-party database service) and transmitted via EmailJS to our inbox at auricmovement@outlook.com.   • Alt text on meaningful images
  • Color contrast that meets or exceeds 4.5:1 for body text
  • Honor of the prefers-reduced-motion setting (animations are minimized when this is enabled in your operating system)
  • Skip-to-main-content link for screen reader and keyboard users
  • Live region announcements (aria-live) for status updates
  • Labeled form fields with clear error messages
  • Touch targets that meet the 44×44px minimum on mobile

4. Venue Accessibility

Our events take place at carefully sourced third-party venues across New York City. Accessibility features vary by location. If you have specific mobility, hearing, vision, sensory, or other accessibility needs, please email auricmovement@outlook.com before booking so we can confirm what the venue can accommodate and help you decide whether the event will work for you.
